## Cover Letter Principles

### All tracks:

* Open with one sentence stating the role
and why Charles specifically fits it
* Never open with "I am writing to apply"
* Reference the company by name and
show awareness of their work or projects
* One specific example of relevant achievement
* Close with clear next step — request
for interview

### Track 1 (Engineering):

* Lead with a specific technical achievement
from MT5 or Lobi Eco
* Show understanding of their tech stack
* Mention Claude Code if they use Anthropic
tooling or AI agents

### Track 2 (Product):

* Lead with a business outcome, not
a technical detail
* Reference PM background briefly
* Show understanding of their product
challenges

### Track 3 (Rail/Civils):

* Open by referencing the specific
infrastructure programme or project
* Name the COSS/PC background early —
this is the differentiator
* Show awareness of Network Rail standards
or relevant regulatory framework

### Track 4 (Doc Control):

* Reference the specific EDMS platform
they use by name in first paragraph
* Mention Tavistock \& Portman archive
project as primary evidence
* Mention Consepsys certification
explicitly by name

to 3.0/5 acceptable given direct cert match.

roles is rare. Score generously where

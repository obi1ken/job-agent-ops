# Glossary

## Rail Operations
- **COSS** — Controller of Site Safety. Responsible for safe system of work on or near the line.
- **PC** — Protection Controller. Senior to COSS; manages multi-group line blockages.
- **SSOW** — Safe System of Work. Documented safety method for working on or near the railway.
- **GERT8000-HB7/HB8** — Network Rail Rule Book modules governing COSS and PC procedures.
- **Sentinel** — Network Rail competency management system. Every worker needs a valid Sentinel card.
- **NR3180** — Network Rail form for line blockage/possession documentation.
- **Possession** — Agreed period when a section of line is blocked for engineering work.
- **OTP** — On-Track Plant. Machinery operating on the railway infrastructure.
- **RRV** — Road Rail Vehicle. Vehicle capable of operating on both road and rail.
- **SMSTS** — Site Management Safety Training Scheme. Construction health and safety qualification.

## Document Control
- **EDMS** — Electronic Document Management System (Aconex, Asite, Procore, Viewpoint, Dalux).
- **ISO 19650** — International standard for managing information over the whole life cycle of a built asset.
- **Golden Thread** — Building Safety Act requirement to maintain accurate, accessible building information.
- **BIM** — Building Information Modelling. Digital representation of physical and functional building characteristics.
- **Transmittal** — Formal document issue record with revision, status, and distribution details.
- **Distribution Matrix** — Document defining who receives which documents at which revision stage.
- **Consepsys** — Document control training and certification provider (Charles holds this cert).

## AI / Tech
- **LLM** — Large Language Model.
- **MLOps** — Machine Learning Operations. Practices for deploying and maintaining ML models.
- **LLMOps** — LLM-specific operations and evaluation tooling.
- **HITL** — Human-in-the-Loop. Human approval step within an automated pipeline.
- **Agentic** — AI system capable of autonomous action and tool use.
- **SP-API** — Amazon Selling Partner API used in Lobi Eco agent.

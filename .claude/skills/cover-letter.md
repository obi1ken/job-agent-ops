# Skill: Cover Letter

## Purpose
Generate a tailored cover letter matched to the selected CV track and the specific JD.

## When to invoke
After CV track is selected and CV variant is drafted. Always generate alongside the CV.

## How to apply
1. Confirm which track is selected
2. Read modes/_profile.md cover letter principles for that track
3. If Track D: EDMS platform detection MUST be completed first — name the platform in para 1
4. Open with one sentence stating the role and why Charles specifically fits it
5. Never open with "I am writing to apply"
6. Reference the company by name — show awareness of their work or context
7. One specific proof point (from the track's proof points in _profile.md)
8. Close with clear next step — request for interview
9. 1 page maximum
10. Strip forbidden phrases (passionate about, leveraged, spearheaded, etc.)
11. Hold for Charles approval. Never submit.

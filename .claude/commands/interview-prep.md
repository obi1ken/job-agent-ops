# Command: /interview-prep

Generate a full interview prep pack for a confirmed interview.

## Usage
/interview-prep [company] [role] [optional: interview date]

## What it does
Invokes the interview-prep skill. Pulls submitted JD and CV version, researches
company, generates questions and model answers, produces prep pack.

## Output
- Company brief (key facts, recent projects, culture signals)
- Likely interview questions (from JD + submitted CV variant)
- Model answers using Charles's actual experience
- Delivered as formatted output; Phase 2 delivers via Discord
